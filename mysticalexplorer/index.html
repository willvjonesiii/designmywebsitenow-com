About Dr. Lee
"My goal is to leave the world a legacy of understandings about the spirit world — gathered through 25 years of direct, first-hand experience."

Dr. Michael Lee is a scientist and mystic who has dedicated over two decades to the rigorous exploration of non-physical reality. Since 2000, he has practiced astral projection, lucid dreaming, and out-of-body travel as primary research methodologies — logging thousands of journeys across different planes of consciousness.

His work spans solo exploration, guided soul retrievals, ITC (Instrumental TransCommunication) research, and systematic documentation of what he identifies as the spirit world. He is considered a bona fide expert in astral projection and afterlife exploration, sharing his findings through YouTube, podcast appearances, and the live ITC Web Station.
